WE ARE CREATIVE

We humans were born to create. That is our purpose, our prime directive. When we are not creating, we are not acting in accordance with our prime directive, and it becomes difficult or impossible to live a fulfilling life. To be happy.Traditionally and evolutionarily speaking, men created shelter, sustenance, business and wealth, while women created babies, which is to say human life. Going back to the dawn of time, back when our ancient ancestors were first blessed with self-aware consciousness, they have imagined what they were going to create, then they created it.

An enormous building that displays the creators’ unbelievable mastery of physics. A novel, read by millions, that shows the human condition in America during one of its toughest eras. Symphonies, composed hundreds of years ago, that still send intense chills down people’s spines. These are all masterpieces. Invaluable contributions to the human race.
